Welcome

Farm Bay is an exclusive Agro-business situated 40km North East of Harare, in Goromonzi, Mashonaland East. Farm Bay has vested interests in local & global export produce & processing.

We are commited to healthy living and our goal is to provide the freshest, healthiest and nutritious foods whilst sustaining our farm environment, employee welfare and the community at large.
